About me

I’m 16 years old from Gothenburg, Sweden currently studying at Amerikanska Gymnasiet. I have previously studied at Fridaskolan at Hisingen, which was a good school where I studied for 4 years. What was so special about that school was that it prepared the students to be independent. For example, the teacher did not say if you had homework because it was your own responsibility to find it out. Another thing that marks the school is that we were about 72 people in each class. The 72 students were divided into three groups. After every break, these groups mixed with each other so the whole class would get a strong connection.
